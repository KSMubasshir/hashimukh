Welcome To Hashimukh

Hashimukh Shomaj Kallayan Shongstha is a non-profit social welfare organization working solely for the betterment of underprivileged children towards a quality life. It is supported constantly by its advisory board and executive committee with guidelines, finances, encouragements, and ideas which drive the organization to better performance each day.

Join Us Now

Mission

Bring positive and sustainable change by giving transformative education to the less privileged young members of society, who have the potential to change the world for the better if given the right, skill set, opportunity, and platform

Vision

Focus on the innovative educational system to transform disadvantaged young members of society into committed professionals with an adequate moral sense.

Objectives

Ensure basic education for the young members of the society
Ensure capacity or skill development
Support in an emergency crisis situation in our country
Motivate the youth of our country to actively participate in volunteering work to give back to the community
Make young members of society well aware of our history, culture, and customs and make them compassionate
Build confidence among the slum families and help them to dream for a better life
